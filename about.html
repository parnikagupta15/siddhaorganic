Our Roots Run Deep

From a Single Seed to a National Movement

Siddha Organic began with a simple philosophy: 'Sehet' vahi, jaha 'Shudhta' sahi. We have always believe in organic farming so came with an idea of farming in our huge acres of ancestral farms whose enriched soil has always resulted in cultivation of healthy crops. Born from a desire to reclaim traditional, chemical-free farming in our family's ancestral lands, our journey is a testament to sustainable health. We are not just a farm; we are a promise to the future, ensuring every grain, spice, and oil is a gift from nature, untouched by toxins.

Our Pillars of Purity: Farming Techniques

Holistic Crop Rotation

We rotate crops seasonally to naturally replenish soil nutrients, break pest cycles, and prevent erosion. This eliminates the need for chemical fertilizers and promotes long-term soil health.

Bio-Pesticides & Companion Planting

We use only natural, indigenous solutions like *Neem oil* and introduce beneficial insects to maintain ecological balance. Companion planting naturally protects our vulnerable crops.

Efficient Drip Irrigation

Water is precious. We employ advanced drip irrigation and rainwater harvesting techniques to ensure maximum efficiency and responsible usage, minimizing our ecological footprint.
